About Me

I worked with Typescript, Angular, Socket.io, and Sails.js to help develop the Captel application within my most recent position as a Junior Software Engineer. I have created several full stack web applications in React with Ruby on Rails as seen within the projects below.

For the past few years, I have self-hosted my own blog website, 'www.musimatic.xyz', and my own art website, 'www.sambanya.com' all by myself. I have since moved the blog site itself to the 'Render deployed website, 'https://musimatic.onrender.com/' for reference, and am planning on making a 'LinkTree' style website for my art and music content to be placed onto the 'www.sambanya.com' website. With a bit of self-driven aptitude and research into how to deploy websites with Nginx and private VPS's on multiple providers (Digital Ocean, Vultr, etc), I was able to transform my ideas into the great projects that you can see below. I am a huge fan of Linux as I have deployed and utilized several Linux distributions including Lubuntu, Debian, Devuan, Gentoo, Fedora, Manjaro, and Linux Mint on several personal machines and servers. I am a proud GNU Emacs user, and am on a never-ending quest on how to use it for everything within my daily workflow. I have used several web development IDE's such as IntelliJ WebStorm and VS Code to create and maintain projects as well. I am also a team player and have excellent verbal and written communication skills. I love learning new tech skills to apply to my current position, and am passionate in delivering the best quality products for our customers.
